Our Goals

Ship a Spectacle 360 to Every Home In America

Imagine being on the basketball court of your favorite NBA team during a game, but from your living room. Now instead of paying for expensive tickets for an average seat, you can be closer to the action from the comfort of your home. How about shopping for new clothes, jewlrey, and even cars or homes with a first-person perspective in your house. We want everyone in America to be able to do this.

Bring Spectacle 360 to Movie Theaters and Amusement Parks

Just as RealD 3D controls 3D movies, we want to control 360 degree movies. When 360 degree movies come to theaters there will need to be a product that can handle rotating frames. We are it.

Bring Spectacle 360 All Over the World

Citizens of Spain want to see attactions in the US as much as American citizens want to see attractions in Spain. Bringing Spectacle 360 to these countries allows for this.

Enable moving through videos

We are currently creating software that takes 360 video one step further. Walking through one video into another would create a realworld virtualworld. Record video/photos in one spot and then record video/photos in a spot a few feet away. Now we can use GPS to navigate between these videos. Partnering with Google Earth, we could walk anywhere in the world from our living rooms.
