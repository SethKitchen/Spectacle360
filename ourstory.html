Our Story

The Idea

Spectacle360 was thought up at an engineering job site in July 2015. The job site had spectacular breakout products, the most relevant being Google Glass. Google Glass' innovative take on viewing digital displays at eye level was central to Spectacle360s conception.

The job site was also where 360 video was witnessed by us for the first time. 360 video allows choices to be made while watching a video.Now the user is allowed to make choices about how the story unfolds. The user becomes a part of the video as a bystander. But when watching on a phone there is still something missing. The user is only half there because the real world still borders the story. To dive completely into the story, something was needed to take away that border. Spectacle360 was thought up to bring the two together and to place audiences even closer to the action.
